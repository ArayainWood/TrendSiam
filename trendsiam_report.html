📋 About This Report

TrendSiam Daily Intelligence Report provides comprehensive analysis of trending digital content across Thailand's digital landscape. This report aggregates and analyzes viral content patterns, audience engagement metrics, and emerging topics to deliver actionable market intelligence for content creators, marketing professionals, brand managers, and digital strategists.

Our proprietary analysis system processes publicly available trending data to identify content performance patterns, audience preferences, and market opportunities. Each report covers a 7-day analysis period and includes detailed breakdowns of the most significant trending stories, their performance metrics, and strategic insights for stakeholders in Thailand's digital economy.

🎯 Scoring Framework

The TrendSiam Popularity Score is a proprietary metric ranging from 0-100 that evaluates trending content performance across multiple dimensions:

  • Audience Reach (30 points): View count analysis with benchmarking against platform averages
  • Engagement Quality (50 points): Like-to-view ratios, comment density, and audience interaction patterns
  • Viral Indicators (20 points): Presence of trending keywords, hashtags, and cross-platform mentions

Our rule-based scoring model ensures consistent, objective evaluation while AI-powered analysis provides contextual insights into content performance drivers and audience appeal factors.

⚠️ Data Sources & Disclaimer

Data Collection: All analysis is based on publicly available data accessed through official YouTube API services in compliance with platform terms of service. Content metrics include view counts, engagement ratios, publication timestamps, and metadata available through public channels.

Usage Guidelines: This report is intended for informational and strategic planning purposes. All content analysis respects creator intellectual property rights and platform community guidelines. Trend analysis reflects past performance and does not guarantee future content success.
